Our Sweet Legacy

Welcome to Sri Durga Sweets, a name that has been synonymous with taste, tradition, and trust in Bhadrachalam for over 30 years. Situated on Indira Market Road, our shop is known for its wide range of traditional sweets and homemade delicacies that bring joy to every occasion.

Our Journey

The roots of Sri Durga Sweets go back to the 1960s, when Shri Birru Srisailam began making sweets by hand and selling them locally with a strong focus on quality and authenticity. His passion and dedication to the craft earned him great respect in the community.

In 1995, after years of hard work, he established a permanent shop in Indira Market, Bhadrachalam, laying the foundation for what would become a household name. He later handed over the business to his son, Birru Sudhakar, who continues to carry forward the legacy with the same commitment to taste, hygiene, and customer satisfaction.

Our Commitment

At Sri Durga Sweets, we believe in preserving the authentic flavors of tradition while maintaining the highest standards. Whether it's a festival, celebration, or daily treat, our sweets are made to bring happiness to your plate.
